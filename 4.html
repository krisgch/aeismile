Aei and Smile Story

Saatchi Gallery, London 2018

This pic was taken outside Saatchi gallery after we ate our... I don't even know what it is.. Some meat in buns.. Not the best but the oysters were sooooo refreshing.

Looking at this makes me think of the times when we were still shy. When we kept our feelings to ourselves. Makes me wish I had the courage to ask if the seat next to you is taken.. Can I sit next to you? Can I stay here and make you laugh?

Guess it all works out in the end. You have no choice but to laugh now. Hope I don't trouble you na kub.
